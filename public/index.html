google-home-notifier webservice

A simple web service for playing synthesized speech or media files on Google Home devices.

See: https://github.com/watusi/google-home-notifier

Demos:

Please open this document by accessing the service - not by opening this file directly in your browser. e.g. http://localhost/google-home-notifier

Although meant primarly for use as a web service, you can play speech or files by submitting a form from a webpage. View the HTML source for details.

These demos also serve as a simple way to verify that the service is properly installed and running.
